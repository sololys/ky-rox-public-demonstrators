# Public Boundary

This repository is an export surface. It is not a development workspace, private
staging area, quarantine, or archive of internal work.

## Allowed

- Frozen public reference language.
- Sanitized demonstrators with declared non-goals.
- Public fixtures, expected outputs, and exact-revision checksums.
- Static media without embedded scripts, external references, or hidden payloads.
- Release attestations for an exact public tree.

## Prohibited

- Protected architecture, algorithms, or control laws.
- Operational parameters, thresholds, timing, routing, or decision tables.
- Production hardware, interlock, deployment, or actuation details.
- Internal history schemas, private records, or chain implementations.
- Patent-sensitive mechanisms or unpublished protocols.
- Secrets, credentials, private repository names, or confidential roadmaps.
- Archives, binaries, notebooks, submodules, symlinks, or generated bundles.

## Publication rule

Real exploratory work remains outside the public repository. A sanitized export is
reviewed locally and privately before its first public push. Public CI is an
integrity check, not a confidentiality boundary.

Unknown content fails closed.
